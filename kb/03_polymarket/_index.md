## How It Works

Polymarket is a prediction market using:

1. **CLOB** - Central Limit Order Book
2. **YES/NO tokens** - Binary outcomes
3. **AMM** - Automated market making for liquidity

See [how-it-works.md](how-it-works.md) for details.
